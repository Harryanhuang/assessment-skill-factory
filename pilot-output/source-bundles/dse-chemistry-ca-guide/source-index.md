## Scope summary

This guide defines the HKDSE Chemistry curriculum framework, learning objectives, topic structure, teaching guidance, and public assessment design. It is **not** a short syllabus; it mixes curriculum aims, strand-level learning objectives, detailed topic content, assessment guidance, and appendices. For skill-building purposes the relevant extracts are:

- Curriculum framework and learning objectives (Chapter 2)
- Mandatory topics 1–12 (Chapter 2.3.1)
- Elective topics 13–15, choose any two (Chapter 2.3.2)
- Investigative Study (Chapter 2.3.3)
- Assessment design, assessment objectives, and public examination structure (Chapter 5)
- Experimental techniques appendix (Appendix 2)
